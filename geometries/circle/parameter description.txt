f: facet fraction.
   The ratio of facet diameter to spherical nanoparticle diameter.
   Range: 0.1 - 0.4

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
D (nm): Sphere's Diameter.
        Range: 40-100nm
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
t (nm): gap thickness. 
        Range: 0.75-6nm
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
n: gap refractive index. 
   Range: 1.25-2

The facet has 5nm radius rounding applied to the edges.
